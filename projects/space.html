Space

Creator: Augusto Mota Pinheiro
Start Date: 03/11/2019
End Date: 05/05/2020
Medium: Blender

I've always looked to the stars in amazement: knowing there's almost an infinite number of worlds that we could explore if only we had a way of getting there... Admiring every shining point on the world's ceiling and I can just envision what planets orbit around that star.

Despite not being able to visit these faraway worlds, I can still have an idea of what our own motherland looks like from a distant point of view. Starting with the Earth, I got inspired to do Mars, our sister-planet, and finally did the Moon to complete the "astral family".

All textures come from NASA and other open license sources.

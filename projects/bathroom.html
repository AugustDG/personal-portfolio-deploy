BATHROOM

Creator: Augusto Mota Pinheiro
Start Date: 03/09/2017
End Date: N/A
Medium: Blender

An inspiration that came to me after looking at various Blender tutorials on YouTube™. Aiming for photorealism, Bathroom is a "in progress" project that I will continue to work on for the foreseeable future.

Some 3D models are not mine, they come from CC (Creative Commons) sources. The bar of soap, sink, tap, water, mirror and towel are made by me.
